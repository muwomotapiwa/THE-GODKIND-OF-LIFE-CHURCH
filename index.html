Who We Are

New Here?

What should I wear?
Come as you are! Most people dress casually, but you'll see everything from jeans to suits.
Is there childcare?
Yes! We have excellent programs for children ages 0-12 during all Sunday services.
How long is the service?
Services typically last about 90 minutes with worship, teaching, and response time.
What's the music like?
Our worship is contemporary and vibrant, with a full band leading us in praise.
