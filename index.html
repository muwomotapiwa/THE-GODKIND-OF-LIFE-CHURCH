Who We Are

The GodKind of Life exists to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by loving God, serving others, and transforming our world.

Our Beliefs

  • We believe in the Bible word ~ 2 Timothy 3Vs16
  • In God in three (trinity) ~ Ephesians 4Vs5–6
  • We believe in the deity ~ John 1vs1–4 | John 10Vs32 | Hebrews 1Vs1–5
  • Birth of Jesus ~ Mathew 1Vs18–25
  • Death ~ John 19Vs30–35
  • Resurrection John ~ 20Vs25–29
  • Salvation ~ Romans 1Vs16
  • Miracles ~ Mark 19Vs26
  • Rapture ~ 1 Thessalonians 4Vs16
  • Impact of the Spirit
  • Life of Christ ~ John 10Vs10 | John 5Vs9–13
  • Victory in Christ ~ Corinthians 14
  • Made Witnesses ~ Acts 1Vs8
  • Sanctification ~ Romans 5Vs17

Our Story

Vision

Participators in the heavenly calling by soul winning, beautifying lives, equipping them, and sending them to impact their communities, cities, and the world at large with the Gospel of Our Lord Jesus Christ.

Mission

To be the center and source of life, victory, and solutions that change status quos, as we give life to everyone and everything everywhere.

Objectives

  • Paint the World Jesus – Spread the Gospel
  • Win souls and change them
  • Reveal identity in Christ
  • Send others to win others
  • Live victoriously and teach others to do the same

Our Values

  • Truthful & God-fearing
  • Integrity & Honesty
  • Transparency & Dedication
  • Responsibility & Team Work
  • Accountability & Excellence

Motto

“Giving life daily, winning daily.”

YondaISM

YondaISM is an outreach expression of The GodKind of Life Church focused on training, activating, and launching believers into their unique callings and kingdom assignments.

With a passion for global impact, YondaISM exists to equip men and women with the knowledge of God’s Word, the empowerment of the Holy Spirit, and the discipline to lead transformational ministries in every sphere of influence.

Through dynamic teachings, practical ministry sessions, and hands-on mentorship, students of YondaISM return to their communities bold, Spirit-led, and full of divine purpose. We prioritize spiritual impartation, doctrinal accuracy, and the manifestation of the supernatural as a lifestyle.

Whether you're a pastor, youth leader, entrepreneur, or visionary, YondaISM is your launchpad to walking fully in your divine mandate.
